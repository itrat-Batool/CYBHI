# **Evolo AI CYBHI Functionality Incident Reporting: User Testing Guide (iOS)**

## **Part A: Installation & Setup**

**Step 1: Confirm Apple ID**

- Use the same Apple ID email address you provided to the Evolo team.
- On your iPhone/iPad, go to **Settings → \[Your Name\] → iCloud & Media Purchases** to confirm.

**Step 2: Install TestFlight**

- Open the **App Store**.
- Search for **TestFlight** (Apple’s official beta testing app).
- Download and install it.

**Step 3: Accept the Test Invitation**

- Open the invitation link provided by the App Store.
- Ensure Safari (or your browser) is logged in with the same Apple ID.
- Tap **Accept**.
- TestFlight will open and show the Evolo AI – Instructor App.
- In TestFlight, tap **Install** (or **Update** if already installed).

### **Step 4: Testing a New Build**

If you receive a new build, open TestFlight, update the required application, and the latest build will be available for testing.

## **Part C: Feedback Guidance**

We want your feedback to go beyond “pass/fail.” Please share:

- **Bugs:** Anything broken, confusing, or not working as expected.
- **UI Improvements:** Suggestions to make the app easier to use (button placement, wording, colors).
- **Enhancements:** Ideas for new features or improvements that would help instructors in CYBHI workflows.

## **Part D: Why Your Feedback Matters**

Your testing helps ensure the Evolo AI CYBHI app is:

- ✅ Compliant with CYBHI documentation and reporting standards.
- ✅ Usable for real instructors in schools and adult education programs.
- ✅ Improved with real-world input on incident reporting, chatbot responsiveness, and reporting workflows.

Every bug you catch, every UI tweak you suggest, and every enhancement idea you share helps us build a tool that truly supports staff and students.
