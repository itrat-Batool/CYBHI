**Evolo AI CYBHI Functionality Incident Reporting: User Testing Guide**

**Part A: Installation & Setup**

**Step 1: Log in with the correct Gmail account**

- Use the **same Gmail address** you provided to the Evolo team.
- Open the **Google Play Store** on your Android device.
- Confirm you are logged in with the correct account under **Profile → Manage your Google Account**.

**Step 2: Locate the Evolo AI CYBHI Instructor App**

- In the Play Store search bar, type: **“Evolo AI Instructor”**.
- Verify the publisher: **Yusair, Inc**.
- Confirm the app name: **Evolo AI – Instructor App**.
- If already installed, you may see **Open/Update**. If not, tap **Install**.

**Step 3: Accept the Internal Test Invitation**

- Open the invitation link provided by Evolo.
- Ensure your browser (e.g., Chrome) is logged in with the same Gmail account.
- Click **Accept Invitation**.
- A confirmation page will appear with a **Download on Google Play** option.

**Step 4: Download the Internal Beta**

- Tap **Download on Google Play**.
- You’ll be redirected to the Play Store with the app labeled **Internal Beta**.
- Tap **Update** (if installed) or **Install** (if new).

**Step 5: Verify Installation**

- Once installed, open the app.
- Confirm the banner: **“You’re an internal tester. This app may be unstable.”**
- You are now ready to begin functionality testing.

**Part C: Feedback Guidance**

We want your feedback to go beyond “pass/fail.” Please share:

- **Bugs**: Anything broken, confusing, or not working as expected.
- **UI Improvements**: Suggestions to make the app easier to use (button placement, wording, colors).
- **Enhancements**: Ideas for new features or improvements that would help instructors in CYBHI workflows.

**Part D: Why Your Feedback Matters**

Your testing helps ensure the Evolo AI CYBHI app is:

- **Compliant** with CYBHI documentation and reporting standards.
- **Usable** for real instructors in schools and adult education programs.
- **Improved** with real-world input on incident reporting, chatbot responsiveness, and reporting workflows.

Every bug you catch, every UI tweak you suggest, and every enhancement idea you share helps us build a tool that truly supports staff and students.

**Note:** Please use version **1.19.0** to test the app. If you receive a pop-up on your device stating that the voice chat does not support speech recognition, kindly be informed that our team is currently working on an alternative solution to support your device.
